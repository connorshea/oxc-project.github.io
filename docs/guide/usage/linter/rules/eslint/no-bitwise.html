eslint/no-bitwise Restriction ​

What it does ​

Disallow bitwise operators

Why is this bad? ​

The use of bitwise operators in JavaScript is very rare and often & or | is simply a mistyped && or ||, which will lead to unexpected behavior.

Example ​

javascript
var x = y | z;
